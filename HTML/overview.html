Overview

At a Glance

We specialize in transforming the limitless power of the sun into innovative, efficient solutions that power homes, businesses, and industries.

Solex Energy Limited, based in Surat, has been at the forefront of sustainable energy solutions since 1995. As the first Indian solar brand to be listed on NSE Emerge (stock code: SOLEX), we have built a strong reputation for providing high-quality photovoltaic modules at competitive prices. Our expertise includes advanced solar module manufacturing and comprehensive EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) services, allowing us to serve a diverse range of industries and communities committed to a greener future.

At Solex Energy, we leverage decades of experience alongside cutting-edge technologies like automation, robotics, and artificial intelligence to create precision-engineered solutions. Our state-of-the-art Global Giga Factory employs industry-leading practices such as Lean Manufacturing, Six Sigma, and Total Quality Management to guarantee outstanding quality, efficiency, and sustainability. With a firm dedication to innovation and customer satisfaction, Solex Energy is actively shaping the future of renewable energy, empowering both industries and individuals to embrace clean, reliable energy solutions.
